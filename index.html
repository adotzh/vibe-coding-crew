What this is

A tiny build lab.

Vibe Coding is a one-day lab for builders who would rather automate a workflow than complain about it. It's equal parts product sprint, automation dojo, and intentional hangout.

You show up with one gnarly manual process. We surround you with mentors, a curated AI stack, and tiny squads that keep you moving until the workflow runs on its own.

Micro-groups. 25 humans max, intentionally mixed across engineering, design, ops, and content.

Real delivery. Walk out with a working assistant, documentation, and a short demo video.

Retreat energy. Good food, zero hustle theater, plenty of corners to pair-program or recharge.

Why it clicks

Collaboration over competition, tiny teams, and a shared library of tools keep everyone building fast without burning out.

Collaboration-first

Every idea gets instant co-founders. Demos happen in circles, not on stages, so feedback feels like a group brain.

Fast building

Two focused build sprints plus expert office hours move you from “maybe” to “it's live” in a single day.

Shared tools

Prompt libraries, workflow templates, and vetted AI services are ready to plug in—no more tab-hunting.

Micro-teams

Teams of 2–4 make decisions instantly, swap roles, and keep momentum even when a blocker appears.

24-hour plan

A day to build something cool, meet great people, and actually enjoy yourself.

10:00 – 10:30

Arrival & Kickoff

  • Coffee plus a rapid intro round
  • Icebreaker: share one manual task you’d automate
10:30 – 11:15

Idea circle

  • 2–3 minute pitches to surface sparks
  • Voting to reveal ideas people want to join or amplify
11:15 – 12:30

Teams & tools

  • Form teams around the highlighted ideas
  • Optional 1–2 minute micro-shares of favorite tools
  • Shared Tool Library available online so everyone stays in sync
12:30 – 13:30

Lunch & plan

  • Finalize roles and the first execution steps
  • Keep the energy up with relaxed networking
13:30 – 15:30

Build Sprint 1

  • Kick off prototyping with rapid iteration
  • Shared learning encouraged—teams can pair up or swap help
  • Mentors on demand for unblock-anything moments
15:30 – 16:00

Break

  • Reset however you like so the afternoon hits with fresh energy
17:00 – 18:00

Build Sprint 2

  • Polish flows, add landing/waitlist, tighten UX
  • Run mini-tests to ensure users can get value right away
18:00 – 19:00

Demo circle

  • Short team presentations with an emphasis on constructive feedback
  • Shared learning mindset over competition
19:00 – 19:30

Reset zone

  • Yoga, mindfulness, or simple relaxation to decompress before celebration
19:30 – 20:30

Final share

  • Teams share insights, lessons learned, and next steps
  • Skill exchange plus contact swaps to keep collaborations alive
20:30 – 21:00

Closing

  • Full-group reflections on the day
  • Map out future collaboration plans before the lights go down

FAQ

Do I need to be technical?

You should be comfortable building things—writing code, designing products, or automating workflows. We pair folks so no one is left solo.

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop, a task you want to automate, and access to any tools or data you need. We provide food, space, and the shared tool library.

Solo or with friends?

Both work. Apply individually and note who you’d like to team up with, or let us match you with complementary skill sets.

What happens after 24 hours?

We keep building together. Expect async check-ins, remote co-working sessions, and pop-up briefs for client work or co-founded projects.
