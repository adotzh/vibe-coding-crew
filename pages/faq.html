FAQ

The quick answers.

Still curious after this? DM us in the chat or email hello@vibecoding.camp. We love nerding out about the details.

Do I need to know how to code?

You should be comfortable making things—shipping products, designing flows, or wiring tools together. If you can explain your process and collaborate, you’ll be fine.

What kind of ideas are you looking for?

We focus on automations that remove painful repeat work for real people: onboarding flows, content ops, research assistants, sales handoffs, little internal tools. Bring something specific.

Can I come with my team?

Yes, but everyone still applies individually. We’ll keep you together or pair you with complementary builders if you’re open to it.

What’s included in the fee?

All meals, snacks, coffee, supplies, venue access, mentor support, templates, and lifetime entry to the private community spaces.

Do you help after the retreat?

Yep. Expect async office hours, shared Kanban boards, and pop-up briefs. Many teams keep iterating and even ship paid client work together.

What if I have to cancel?

Let us know as soon as possible. We can defer you to a future build window or offer a partial refund if we can backfill your spot.

Still unsure?

Ping us in Telegram or email hello@vibecoding.camp with your idea. We’ll tell you honestly if the retreat is a fit.
