The One-Day Build Lab

A 24-hour sprint where a single pain point becomes a working AI solution.

10:00 – 10:30

Kickoff

  • Arrival, intros, and first sparks
  • Share one manual task you want gone
10:30 – 11:15

Idea circle

  • 2–3 min pitches
  • Group voting to surface the most exciting ideas
11:15 – 12:30

Teams & tools

  • Form teams around ideas that match your curiosity
  • Optional tool micro-shares
  • Shared Tool Library available online
12:30 – 13:30

Lunch & plan

  • Finalize roles and task flow
  • Slow networking with your team
13:30 – 15:30

Build sprint 1

  • Rapid prototyping
  • Cross-team help encouraged
  • Mentors available on demand
15:30 – 16:00

Break

Recharge for the second half.

17:00 – 18:00

Build sprint 2

  • Polish flows & UX
  • Add onboarding or waitlist
  • Run quick tests with teammates
18:00 – 19:00

Demo circle

  • Short demos → collaborative feedback
  • No competition — only shared learning
19:00 – 19:30

Reset zone

  • Mindfulness, stretch, tea, whatever helps clear the mind
19:30 – 20:30

Final share

  • Insights and lessons
  • Plans for continuing the build
  • Connections for future collaborations
